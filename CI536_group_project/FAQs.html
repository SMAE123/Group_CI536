What is student Marketplace?

Studen Marketplace is a place where to sell and buy products for students in Brighton area with a reasonable price.

Is account registration required?

Account registration at Login and Registratin link area in the home page is only required if you will be selling or buying products. This ensures a valid account details for all students involved in any transactions.

Can we sell products to Markerplace?

Yes you can sell as long as you are a member of students.

Can we buy products from Markerplace?

Yes you can buy as long as you are a member of students.

What are the payment options?

The best way to transfer funds is via Paypal. This secure platform ensures timely payments and a secure environment.
